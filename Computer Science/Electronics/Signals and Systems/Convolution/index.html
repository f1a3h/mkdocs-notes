    Convolution

    Properties of Convolution¶

    1. identity element: \((x \ast \delta)[n] = x[n]\)
    2. \((x \ast \delta)[n-N] = x[n-N]\)
    3. commutative: \(x \ast h = h \ast x\)
    4. distributive: \(x\ast (h_1 + h_2) = x \ast h_1 + x \ast h_2\)
